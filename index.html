Capabilities

Built with AI at
every step

Five intelligent features working together to make civic grievance filing fast, fair, and frictionless.

Feature 1

Confidence Thresholding

Every classification comes with a confidence score. Low-confidence complaints are flagged for human review automatically.

Feature 2

Smart Autocomplete

As you type, FAISS-powered semantic search surfaces the most relevant complaint categories in real time.

Feature 3

Resolution Time Prediction

A trained ML model predicts how long your complaint will take to resolve, based on category and department data.

Feature 4

Sentiment-Aware Feedback

Our feedback system analyses the sentiment of your response and flags unresolved or escalated complaints automatically.

Feature 5

Conversational Filing Bot

A guided chat interface collects your complaint details step by step — no complex forms, no confusion.
